Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • Canola Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • White Onion
  • Celery
  • Ginger
  • Coleslaw
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Stir-fry Noodles
  • Tamari
  • Mirin
    •

    Steps

    1. Heat oils on high heat
    2. Add onion, celery, and Ginger
    3. Cook and stir for 1 minute
    4. Add coleslaw and Sprouts
    5. Cook and stir for 2 minutes
    6. Add noodles and cook and stir for 1 minute
    7. Add tamari and Mirin
    8. Cook and stir for 2 minutes
